about us

why choose us?

Classical Store brings out the latest international trends in the form of easy-going, comfortable and casual clothing. The Classical Store collections are designed to dress Men, without age being an obstacle to choosing their wardrobe.

Classical Store evolves, in tune with its customers, always attentive to new technologies, social movements and the latest artistic or musical trends. All this is reflected not only in its designs, but also in its shops. In the purest style of Palm Springs, a mythical city in California, the offer is constantly being renewed. Twice a week, all stores are indeed replenished.
